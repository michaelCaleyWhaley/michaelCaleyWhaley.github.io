About Us

Providing cost effective air conditioning solutions that cool and heat for both commercial and domestic customers alike...this is what Caltech is all about.

Pete Caley set up Caltech in 2003 for this purpose and since then Pete and his team have installed and currently maintain hundreds of air conditioning systems throughout Yorkshire and the Humber region.

Peter says "many of our customers have been with Caltech for a large number of years and tell us that they really appreciate seeing regular trusted faces during our service visits".

Caltech are an F-GAS Refcom registered company no. REF1001800 and all our work is fully insured and comes with our no quibble three year guarantee, so you can rest assured that the trust you place in us, is well founded.

Whether you're interested in a new installation, a replacement system, maintenance or a repair visit, we are here to provide a relaxed, friendly source of information and advice.
